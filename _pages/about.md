Hey! I am a rising senior at [Princeton University](https://www.princeton.edu/), majoring in computer science and minoring in finance and statistics/machine learning.

This semester, I worked on developing an assertions-based debugging tool for [NVIDIA CUDA-Q](https://developer.nvidia.com/cuda-q), advised by Prof. [Margaret Martonosi](https://mrm.scholar.princeton.edu/). This summer, I am interning at Microsoft Research with the [Software-Hardware Co-Design Lab](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/software-hardware-co-design-sharc/), mentored by Dr. [Sharad Agarwal](https://sharadagarwal.net/).

I am interested in operating systems, computer architecture, and cybersecurity. When time allows, I enjoy [writing](https://www.dailyprincetonian.com/staff/coco-gong), swimming, and instant photography.
